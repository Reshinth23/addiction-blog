Alcohol Addiction

Alcohol addiction is a serious issue that can have devastating effects on individuals and their families. This section discusses the causes, effects, and recovery options for alcohol addiction.

Common Symptoms of Alcohol Addiction

  • Increased tolerance to alcohol
  • Withdrawal symptoms when not drinking
  • Neglecting responsibilities in favor of drinking
