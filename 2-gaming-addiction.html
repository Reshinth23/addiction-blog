Gaming Addiction

Gaming addiction has become a rising issue in modern times, affecting both mental health and daily life routines. This section explores the causes, effects, and ways to seek help for gaming addiction.

Common Symptoms of Gaming Addiction

  • Preoccupation with gaming
  • Neglecting personal relationships and responsibilities
  • Inability to stop gaming despite negative consequences
